About Us:

Ken Lee, PFA is a Financial Advisor and Tax Planner based in Toronto. I specalize in filing Canadian tax returns for individuals (T1) and not-for-profit organizations (T2). In addition to tax preparation, I aim to guide my clients through all relevant tax provisions to make strategic decisions to minimize tax liability.

I also write MaxMillions with Ken, a biweekly publication that aims to make insights on key and relevant Canadian tax/financial policy accessible to all Canadians. Through my advisory work and writing, I aim to reduce confusion around the Canadian tax system and promote financial literacy.

In my free time, I enjoy listening to Canadian '80s tunes and exploring Toronto. Proudly Canadian — go Leafs! 🍁 I look forward to connecting with you!
